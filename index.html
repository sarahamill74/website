About Me

Creative, Innovative, Strategic

I am a highly intuitive leader who has a long professional history of creating, growing and executing organizational strategy, programming and operations. My passion is creatively solving complex problems, and creating alignment between research, patterns, trends, and mission, to create a clear vision for the future. My strengths include strong leadership, sound judgement, attention to detail, the ability to synthesize and organize complex information, a high standard of personal integrity, deep empathy for others, well-developed emotional intelligence, and a desire to collaborate. Others describe me as grounded and calm with the ability to make others feel at ease. My introspective mind works fast, and I am a curious and constant learner, always eager to develop my skill-set. For many years, I have fostered a passion for organizational and leadership development and human psychology through extensive education, reading, research, listening and practice. I am a servant leader, relationship builder, independent and analytical thinker, experienced in effective communication, and efficient time manager.
