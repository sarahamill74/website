Training Samples

Over my years of professional, educational, and community work, I have had the opportunity to create and lead many trainings. These projects reflect both my expertise in conflict management within the organizational and community contexts and my efforts to increase education around mental and emotional well-being.

I created this training while working for Second Harvest Food Bank of Middle Tennessee. It is a training meant to help food distribution volunteers deal with the conflict that often arises at their agencies among clients.
I helped to create and design this facilitator training for my last organization. In this version, I narrate the training, not scripted, but as I would lead it if it were synchronous learning. This training is primarily done in an online environment.
I created this presentation on mental health resources for the annual “Touring Career Workshop” that takes place in Nashville, Tennessee to support employees of the music and touring industry.
I created this training for the YMCA of Middle Tennessee. It is meant to help membership staff deal productively with conflict that might happen with members. It was designed for a classroom, synchronous learning environment and also has job aids.
